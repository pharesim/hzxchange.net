In order to log into HZxCHANGE, you need to enter the passphrase to your Horizon account. This is normally the string of twelve random words that were assigned to you when you created your account.

If you don't have a Horizon account, you can easily create one by entering some text into the login box and your the system will automatically create one for you.

PLEASE ENSURE THAT YOU KEEP YOUR PASSPHRASE IN A SAFE AND SECURE PLACE AS THERE IS NO WAY TO RETRIEVE A LOST PASSPHRASE.

Entering your passphrase into the login box is 100% safe as we use a process called 'local signing'. This means that nothing is transferred between your computer and the internet. This site never sends requests to other servers, except the HZ server specified in the settings. Your passphrase never leaves your browser.

When you trade on HZxCHANGE, you need to setup deposit addresses for each currency you are trading with.

To do this, you need to click on the blue 'Get Deposit Address' button and wait for the confirmation.

Because HZxCHANGE stores all information securely on the Horizon blockchain instead of server-based wallets, it can sometimes take a bit longer to create your deposit address. During this time, the blue button will display a 'Waiting For Address' message before finally updating to 'Deposit'.

THIS CAN TAKE ANYWHERE FROM 60 SECONDS TO 30 MINUTES BUT IS A ONE-TIME ONLY PROCESS FOR EACH COIN. WE BELIEVE THIS IS A FAIR TRADE OFF FOR THE ADDED SECURITY OF NOT HAVING HACKABLE SERVER-BASED WALLETS.
